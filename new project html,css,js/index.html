Frequently Asked Questions

How is your decaf processed?

We use the Swiss Water Process, a chemical-free method that removes 99.9% of caffeine while preserving the coffee's original flavor profile.

Does decaf really taste as good as regular coffee?

When done right, absolutely! Our careful processing and roasting ensures that our decaf maintains the complex flavors and aromas you love in coffee.

How much caffeine is in your decaf?

Our decaf contains less than 0.1% caffeine - that's about 2-3mg per cup compared to 95mg in regular coffee.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your order, we'll refund it or send you a replacement.
