Frequently Asked Questions

How is your decaf different?

We use the Swiss Water Process, which removes 99.9% of caffeine while preserving the coffee's original flavor profile.

Is decaf coffee healthy?

Yes! Decaf coffee contains the same antioxidants as regular coffee but without the potential side effects of caffeine.

How should I brew your decaf?

Our decaf works great with any brewing method. We recommend a 1:15 coffee-to-water ratio for optimal flavor extraction.
